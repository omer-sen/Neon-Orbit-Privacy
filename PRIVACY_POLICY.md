# Neon Orbit Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

Neon Orbit is a mobile arcade game. This privacy policy explains what information the game uses and how third-party services may process data.

## Information We Store

Neon Orbit stores gameplay preferences and progress locally on your device, including:

- Best score
- Games played
- Obstacles cleared
- Diamonds collected
- Selected themes
- Audio, haptics, and control settings
- Purchase state, such as remove ads

This local gameplay data is stored on your device using Apple system storage. We do not use this data to personally identify you.

## Ads

Neon Orbit uses Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Google may collect or process information such as device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, approximate location, and other data as described in Google's privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob information:
https://support.google.com/admob

## In-App Purchases

Neon Orbit uses Apple StoreKit for in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Apple Privacy Policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Children

Neon Orbit does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will take appropriate action.
